How to Calibrate Compass | Compass Calibration Guide for Iphone and Android

To calibrate a compass, rotate it in a figure-eight motion a few times. Ensure you are in an open area away from metal objects and electronic devices. calibration helps improve the accuracy of your compass readings.

Calibrating a compass is crucial for accurate direction readings. When you use a compass, especially on electronic devices like smartphones and smartwatches, calibration becomes essential. The process involves rotating the device in a figure-eight motion to enhance the precision of the compass sensor.

How to Calibrate Compass on iPhone

To calibrate the compass on your iPhone, follow these steps:

  • 1. Open the "Compass" app or online compass.
  • 2. Rotate your iPhone in a figure-eight motion until the compass arrow turns green.
  • 3. Calibration is complete when the arrow stabilizes, ensuring accurate compass direction readings.

Calibration in Apple Watch

Apple Watch relies on the iPhone's compass, so ensure your iPhone is calibrated first for accurate readings on your Apple Watch.

How to Calibrate Compass on Android

To calibrate the compass on your Android device, use these steps:

  • 1. Open the "Compass" app or Online Compass Tool.
  • 2. Move your phone in a figure-eight motion until the compass calibrates.
  • 3. The compass is calibrated when the readings stabilize, providing accurate direction information.

Calibration in Android Watch and Android TV

For Android watches, follow the same steps as for the phone. For Android TV, ensure that the TV is placed in a location with minimal interference and perform the figure-eight motion for calibration.


This simple calibration process enhances the accuracy of compass direction readings on your devices, ensuring reliability in various applications, including online compass direction services.
